## The Passepartout:


1.  $$\frac{\partial b^\intercal a}{\partial a} = b$$

2.  $$\frac{\partial a^\intercal A a}{\partial a} = (A^\intercal + A)a$$

3.  $$\frac{\partial \log (\det A) }{\partial A}= (A^{-1})^\intercal$$

4.  $$\frac{\partial tr(BA)}{\partial A}= B^\intercal $$
